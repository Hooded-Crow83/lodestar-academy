About Lodestar Academy

Our Mission

No two people are exactly alike, and it is so important to embrace this truth as we nurture our youth. At Lodestar Academy, we recognize and celebrate the uniqueness of each student, encouraging them to take an active role in their learning journey. By allowing students to guide the way, we empower them to reach their fullest potential and uncover abilities they may never have realized they had. This not only fosters their academic development but their emotional well-being, feeding their hearts and minds in ways that are life changing.

  • Small class sizes.
  • Individual learning plans for all students.
  • Life skills and emotional / social learning.
  • Relaxed, cozy atmosphere
  • Transparent pricing.
